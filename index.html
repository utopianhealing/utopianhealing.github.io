utopian healing farm & spa is an imaginary QTBIPOC-centered farm commune and trans-friendly spa located in liminal space (between what was and what’s next) with “next” being a network of farms and spas on borrowed land.

utopian healing is the non-place we must cultivate within our minds and throughout our friendships in these dystopian times - an initiative focused on building community for marginalized identities, food sovereignty, bountiful mental health support and resources, and frequent reflection upon our intentions, priorities, and effectiveness.
