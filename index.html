utopian healing farm & spa is an imaginary farm and spa located in the dreamscape of its community: low-income, disabled, queer, and trans black, indigenous, and people of color in urban settlements. With our zines and kits, we sow our first seeds to cultivate a collective farm in the scorched soil of the present-day dystopia. Whispers among us say that beyond the horizon lies a cooperative of community-owned farms and spas on captive lands.
